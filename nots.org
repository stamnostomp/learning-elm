#+title: Nots

* arithmetic

** division

there is two forms of division in elm

1. "/" for floating point
2. "//" for integer


** infix vs prefix style

*** infix style

more traditional placing the operator in between the numerical arguments


#+begin_src elm
1 + 2
3 : number

42 * 10
420 : number
#+end_src

*** prefix style

more functional placing the operator infront of the numerical arguments

#+begin_src elm
(+) 1 2
3 : number

(*) 42 10
420 : number
#+end_src
